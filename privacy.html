Privacy Policy for SortSmart-SE

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

SortSmart-SE ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access

SortSmart-SE requires access to your device's camera to identify waste items using Machine Learning. All image analysis is performed locally on your device using Google ML Kit. We do not store, save, or transmit any images or video frames captured by the camera to our servers or any third parties.

Local Data

The app saves your "Favorites" and "Recent Searches" locally on your device. This data is not synced to any cloud service and remains under your control.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google ML Kit for image recognition. You can review Google's privacy policy regarding ML Kit on their official website.

3. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any personal data on our servers, we do not retain any of your information. Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear its cache.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from children or any other users.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@sortsmart.se
